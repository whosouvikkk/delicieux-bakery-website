About Us

Since 2008, Délicieux has been the heart of the neighborhood — a place where the smell of fresh bread, the warmth of the oven, and the joy of sharing bring people together. Every recipe is a labor of love.

We source our flour from heritage wheat farms, use free-range eggs, and only the finest Belgian chocolate. Because you deserve nothing less.
